# **App Name**: nxMplisCore Digital Showcase

## Core Features:

- Homepage Highlights: Showcase nxMplisCore’s brand story, industries served, and key technologies like Compute-in-Memory and neuromorphic computing on the homepage.
- About Us Section: Provide detailed information about the company's mission, team, advisors, and investors.
- Technology Overview: Deep dive into nxMplisCore’s core technologies, including compute-in-memory, neuromorphic computing, and analog AI. Use interactive diagrams and animated illustrations to explain complex concepts.
- Industry Solutions Showcase: Showcase nxMplisCore's solutions for healthcare, fintech, automotive, and smart home sectors. Use real-world use cases to illustrate the impact of edge AI applications.
- Career Listings and Application: Dedicated job board with listings, an integrated CV collection form and optional ATS integration to streamline candidate applications.
- Investor and Partnership Funnels: Implement dynamic forms for investor and partner inquiries, ensuring efficient lead capture with data export capabilities.
- AI-Driven Content Recommendations: AI-powered tool for recommending related articles and insights based on user browsing behavior on the blog. It ensures that the output respects privacy regulations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Space Black (#000000) to evoke a futuristic and bold feel.
- Background color: Very dark desaturated grey (#1A1A1A), complementing the Deep Space Black.
- Accent color: Electric Cyan (#7DF9FF) to highlight key elements and CTAs, providing a vivid contrast.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, computerized aesthetic.
- Body: 'Inter' (sans-serif) to maintain legibility and a clean, contemporary look.
- Employ clean, minimalist icons in Electric Cyan, and maintain a consistent style.
- Use section-based storytelling with smooth scroll transitions for an engaging and intuitive user experience.
